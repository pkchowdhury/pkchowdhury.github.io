About Me

Hi, I'm Pallab!

Excellent educator with experience teaching cutting-edge topics like Machine Learning (ML), Deep Learning (DL), Natural Language Processing (NLP), Data Analytics, Embedded Systems, the Internet of Things (IoT), and Semiconductor Research. Proven ability to serve in both technical and leadership capacities; creates an inspiring atmosphere for student growth through teamwork and innovation. Enthusiastic about teaching, motivating students, and fostering creativity.
I am a Full Stack ML & IoT developer with a huge love for Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, NLP, Embedded systems, Internet of things and firmware development. My research interests include problems in computational biology, bioinformatics, NLP, machine learning and deep learning. My vision is to analyze biological data to solve problems and design tools using ML, DL, NLP and Embedded System application. I earned my Bachelors in Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) from Rajshahi University of Engineering and Technology (RUET). Currently, I'm open to work and research opportunities.
